Blue Switch

Blue switches are known for their satisfying "click" and tactile feedback, requiring slightly more force to press than other switches. They are suitable for typists and gamers who want feedback and durability, but the clicking sound can be disruptive in quiet environments.

Red Switch

Red switches are known for their smooth, quiet operation – ideal for gamers and typists who value silent keystrokes. These linear switches require minimal pressure to actuate, reducing fatigue during long gaming sessions or typing marathons. While lacking the tactile feedback of some switches, their responsiveness makes them a popular choice for competitive gaming and quiet environments.
